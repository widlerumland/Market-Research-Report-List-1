<p><p>The digital humidifier market is segmented into two primary types: cool mist and combined mist (cold and warm). Cool mist humidifiers use a fan to disperse a fine mist of water vapor, helping to increase humidity levels without raising the temperature. In contrast, combined humidifiers offer both cold and warm mist options, allowing users to switch between cooler moisture for summer and soothing warm mist during winter. This versatility enhances comfort and caters to diverse user preferences and environmental needs.</p></p>
