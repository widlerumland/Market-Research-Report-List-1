<p><p>Fixed-mount barcode readers are essential for automated data capture in various settings. Laser scanners are known for their precision in reading 1D barcodes over longer distances, making them ideal for high-speed applications. Linear imagers capture images of barcodes linearly, offering better performance on damaged or poorly printed codes. In contrast, 2D imagers can read both 1D and 2D barcodes, including QR codes, providing versatility and enhanced scanning capabilities, suited for a wide range of industrial and retail environments.</p></p>
