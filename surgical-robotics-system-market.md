<p><p>The surgical robotics system market is primarily categorized into open surgery and minimally invasive surgery. Open surgery involves larger incisions and direct access to tissues, offering extensive visibility and maneuverability for surgeons, yet often leading to longer recovery times. In contrast, minimally invasive surgery utilizes robotic systems to perform procedures through small incisions, promoting quicker recovery, reduced pain, and lower risk of infection. Both types cater to different surgical needs, enhancing precision and control during operations.</p></p>
