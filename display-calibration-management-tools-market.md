<p><ul><li>3D LUT Calibration</li><li>3PT/6PT Calibration</li><li>Calibration Based on ICC Profile</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Display calibration management tools help ensure accurate color reproduction across various devices. The 3D LUT Calibration uses three-dimensional look-up tables to adjust colors precisely, enhancing visual fidelity. The 3PT/6PT Calibration method utilizes specific points on a color spectrum to fine-tune display settings, catering to varying needs in color accuracy. Calibration based on ICC Profiles relies on standardized color profiles to maintain consistent color representation across devices, making it crucial for graphic design, photography, and video production industries.</p></p>
