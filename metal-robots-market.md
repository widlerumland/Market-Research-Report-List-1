<p><p>The metal robots market comprises several types, including articulated robots, Cartesian robots, SCARA robots, and others. Articulated robots feature rotational joints, providing flexibility for complex tasks. Cartesian robots use linear movements along axes for precise, straightforward operations, ideal for assembly and material handling. SCARA robots are designed for high-speed tasks with a versatile arm structure, commonly used in pick-and-place applications. Other market types may include Delta robots and collaborative robots, each offering unique functionalities tailored to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
