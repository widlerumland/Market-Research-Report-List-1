<p><ul><li>"Transarterial Radioembolization (TARE)"</li><li>"Selective Internal Radiation Therapy (SIRT)"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radioembolization therapy market encompasses two primary types: Transarterial Radioembolization (TARE) and Selective Internal Radiation Therapy (SIRT). TARE involves delivering radioactive microspheres directly to tumor sites via the arterial supply, targeting liver cancer treatments effectively. SIRT, while similar, emphasizes patient-specific customization of radiation dose and distribution, enhancing therapeutic outcomes. Both methods utilize isotopes to shrink tumors by combining radiation therapy with embolization, offering minimally invasive options for patients with hepatic malignancies while aiming for improved survival rates and quality of life.</p></p>
