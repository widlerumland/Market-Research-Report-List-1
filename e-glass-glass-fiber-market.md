<p><ul><li>Fiber Yarn</li><li>Fiber Roving</li></ul></p>
<p><p>E-glass fiber, known for its excellent electrical insulation and mechanical properties, is primarily categorized into two market types: fiber yarn and fiber roving. Fiber yarn consists of continuous strands of E-glass fibers that are woven into fabrics for applications like composites and insulation. Fiber roving, on the other hand, is a bundle of glass fibers used in various composite processes, providing strength and rigidity. Both types are essential in industries such as construction, automotive, and aerospace for lightweight and durable solutions.</p></p>
