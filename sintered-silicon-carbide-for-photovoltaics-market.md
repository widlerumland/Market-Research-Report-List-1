<p><p>The Sintered Silicon Carbide market for photovoltaics includes two main types: Reaction Bonded Silicon Carbide (RBSC) and Pressureless Sintered Silicon Carbide (PSSC). RBSC is produced through a chemical reaction that bonds silicon carbide particles, offering excellent thermal conductivity and corrosion resistance, making it suitable for solar applications. PSSC, on the other hand, is created through a high-temperature sintering process without pressure, resulting in dense, durable components. Both types enhance the efficiency and longevity of photovoltaic systems.</p></p>
