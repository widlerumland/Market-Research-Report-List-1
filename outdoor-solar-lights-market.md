<p><ul><li>Standalone</li><li>Grid Connected</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The outdoor solar lights market primarily consists of two types: standalone and grid-connected systems. Standalone solar lights operate independently, using solar panels to harness energy, making them ideal for remote areas without electricity. They typically include LED lights and batteries for storage. In contrast, grid-connected solar lights integrate with the electric grid, allowing for dual power sources. This system provides consistent lighting and can draw power from the grid during periods of low solar energy, ensuring reliability and efficiency.</p></p>
