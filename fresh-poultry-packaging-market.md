<p><p>The fresh poultry packaging market comprises several types, including Modified Atmosphere Packaging (MAP), Vacuum Skin Packaging (VSP), and Vacuum Thermoformed Packaging (VTP), along with other methods. MAP enhances shelf life by altering the gas composition inside the package, while VSP provides a tight seal that maintains freshness by removing air. VTP utilizes heat to create a vacuum seal around the product, offering protection and extending usability. Other packaging methods include shrink films and traditional trays, catering to varying consumer needs.</p></p>
