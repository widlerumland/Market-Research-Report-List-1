<p><p>The injectable drug delivery market encompasses various administration routes tailored to target specific body systems. Skin injection involves delivering drugs directly into the dermis for localized effects, often used for vaccines or dermatological treatments. Circulatory/musculoskeletal injection targets systemic circulation or musculoskeletal conditions, delivering medications like steroids directly into joints. Organ injection focuses on specific organs for treatments such as cancer therapies, while central nervous system injection administers drugs like anesthetics or therapeutics directly into the spinal fluid or brain.</p></p>
