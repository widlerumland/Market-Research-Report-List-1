Gas Chromatography-Tandem Mass Spectrometry (GC-MS/MS) provides enhanced detection capabilities, effective for trace analysis. Liquid Chromatography-Tandem Mass Spectrometry (LC-MS/MS) is particularly useful for liquid samples, offering robust quantification and lower detection limits. Together, these technologies provide essential tools for environmental monitoring and regulatory compliance in dioxin analysis.</p></p>
