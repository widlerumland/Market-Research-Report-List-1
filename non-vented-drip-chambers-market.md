<p><p>Non-vented drip chambers, essential in medical and agricultural applications, come in two main types: macro drip and micro drip. Macro drip chambers allow larger droplets to facilitate faster fluid delivery, making them ideal for rapid infusions and large-scale irrigation. In contrast, micro drip chambers utilize smaller droplets for precise and controlled fluid delivery, often used in sensitive agricultural environments and pediatric medical treatments. Both types enhance efficiency and minimize waste, catering to varying needs in fluid management.</p></p>
