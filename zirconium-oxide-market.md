<p><p>The zirconium oxide market primarily comprises two types: Electro Fused Zirconium Oxide and Chemical Zirconium Oxide. Electro Fused Zirconium Oxide is produced through an electrofusion process, resulting in a durable and high-purity material suitable for applications in ceramics, refractories, and abrasives. Chemical Zirconium Oxide, on the other hand, is derived from chemical processes, offering superior stability and versatility for use in dental ceramics, optical applications, and electronics. Both types play crucial roles across various industries, meeting specific material requirements.</p></p>
