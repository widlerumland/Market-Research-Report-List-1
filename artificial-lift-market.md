<p><p>The artificial lift market comprises several types used to enhance oil and gas production. Rod lift employs mechanical rods to lift fluids, while Electrical Submersible Pumps (ESP) use electric motors for efficient fluid extraction. Progressive Cavity Pumps (PCP) provide a continuous flow through a helical rotor. Hydraulic pumps utilize pressurized liquids, and gas lift employs gas injection to reduce fluid density. Plunger lifts use a plunger to increase efficiency. Additionally, other technologies encompass emerging and hybrid solutions tailored to specific well conditions.</p></p>
