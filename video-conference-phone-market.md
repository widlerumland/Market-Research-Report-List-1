<p><strong>The Video Conference Phone Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Endpoint Systems</li><li>Multipoint Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The video conference phone market comprises primarily two types: endpoint systems and multipoint systems. Endpoint systems are individual devices used for one-on-one or small group video calls, typically featuring a camera, microphone, and screen for direct communication. In contrast, multipoint systems enable multiple locations to connect simultaneously, facilitating larger group meetings. These systems often support advanced features like content sharing and interactive collaboration, making them suitable for businesses requiring extensive connectivity and effective communication across various teams or locations.</p></p>
