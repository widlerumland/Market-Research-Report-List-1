<p><p>The anti-diabetic drugs market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Biguanides** improve insulin sensitivity and reduce glucose production in the liver.</p><p>2. **Sulfonylureas** stimulate insulin release from the pancreas.</p><p>3. **Meglitinides** also promote insulin secretion but act faster and for shorter durations.</p><p>4. **Thiazolidinediones** enhance insulin action in muscle and fat tissues.</p><p>5. **Dipeptidyl Peptidase IV Inhibitors** increase insulin levels by preventing the breakdown of incretin hormones.</p><p>6. **α-Glucosidase Inhibitors** delay carbohydrate absorption in the intestines, helping control post-meal blood sugar spikes.</p></p>
