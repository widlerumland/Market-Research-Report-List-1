<p><p>The engineering plastics market is divided into two main types: general engineering plastics and special engineering plastics. General engineering plastics, such as polyamide (Nylon) and polycarbonate, are widely used for their versatility, durability, and mechanical strength in standard applications. In contrast, special engineering plastics, including polyether ether ketone (PEEK) and polyphenylene sulfide (PPS), possess enhanced properties like chemical resistance and high-temperature stability, making them suitable for specialized applications in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
