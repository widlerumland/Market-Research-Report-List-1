<p><strong>The Spandex Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solution Dry Spinning</li><li>Solution Wet Spinning</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spandex fiber market is primarily categorized by production methods. Solution dry spinning involves dissolving spandex in a solvent, then extruding it through a spinneret and drying the fibers, resulting in high elasticity. Solution wet spinning employs a similar dissolving process but uses a liquid coagulation bath to solidify the fibers, producing softer and more flexible materials. Other methods may include melt spinning and other innovative techniques. These processes directly influence the fiber's quality, performance, and applications in textiles.</p></p>
