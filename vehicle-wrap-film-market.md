<p><strong>The Vehicle Wrap Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cast Film</li><li>Calendered Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle wrap film market primarily consists of two types: cast film and calendered film. Cast film is produced by cooling a liquid polymer to form a flexible, high-quality film, offering superior clarity and conformability, making it ideal for complex curves and designs. In contrast, calendered film is created by pressing layers of PVC through rollers, resulting in a thicker, more durable film suitable for flat surfaces and simpler applications. Each type caters to varying performance, aesthetic, and application requirements.</p></p>
