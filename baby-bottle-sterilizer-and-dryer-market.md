<p><p>The baby bottle sterilizer and dryer market features two primary types: electric steam sterilizers and microwave steam sterilizers. Electric steam sterilizers use high-temperature steam to eliminate bacteria and germs, offering efficient and hands-free operation. They often include drying functions, preparing bottles for immediate use. Microwave steam sterilizers, on the other hand, leverage steam generated in a microwave to sterilize bottles quickly. They are compact and portable, making them convenient for travel, but require a microwave for operation. Both types ensure safe feeding for infants.</p></p>
