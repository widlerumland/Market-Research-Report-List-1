<p><p>The hydro-raking services market is primarily divided into hydraulic and mechanical types. Hydraulic hydro-raking utilizes high-pressure water jets to dislodge and collect aquatic vegetation and debris, making it effective for delicate environments. Mechanical hydro-raking, on the other hand, employs heavy machinery equipped with rakes and scoops to physically remove materials from water bodies. Both methods aim to enhance water quality and ecosystem health, but they cater to different project requirements and operational conditions.</p></p>
