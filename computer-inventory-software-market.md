<p><p>Computer inventory software comes in two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access it via the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and automatic updates. This model reduces IT infrastructure costs and simplifies maintenance. In contrast, on-premise software is installed locally on company servers, providing greater control over data and security. While it may require higher upfront investments and ongoing maintenance, it is preferred by organizations needing strict compliance or customization.</p></p>
