<p><p>The abrasive materials market is categorized into three main types: bonded, coated, and superabrasive. Bonded abrasives consist of abrasive particles securely held in a matrix, ideal for grinding and cutting applications. Coated abrasives feature abrasive grains adhered to a backing material, commonly used in sanding and finishing tasks. Superabrasives, made from materials like diamond or cubic boron nitride, offer exceptional hardness and efficiency, making them suitable for high-performance machining and precision applications. Each type serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
