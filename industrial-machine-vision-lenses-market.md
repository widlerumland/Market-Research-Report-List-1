<p><strong>The Industrial Machine Vision Lenses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-Mount</li><li>CS-Mount</li><li>F-Mount</li><li>S-Mount</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial machine vision lenses are categorized into several mount types based on their compatibility with cameras and applications. C-Mount lenses have a standard screw thread, suitable for various industrial uses. CS-Mount lenses, a smaller variant, are often used in compact systems. F-Mount lenses are primarily associated with digital single-lens reflex (DSLR) cameras. S-Mount (or M12) lenses are compact and popular for small cameras. Other types encompass specialty and custom mounts, catering to unique industrial requirements and applications.</p></p>

<p><p>The Industrial Machine Vision Lenses Market serves two primary applications: area scan cameras and line scan cameras. Area scan cameras capture two-dimensional images by exposing an entire scene at once, making them ideal for applications like quality inspection and barcode reading. In contrast, line scan cameras acquire images one line at a time, offering high-resolution imaging for scanning moving objects, such as textiles or packaging. Both types enhance automation and clarity in industrial processes, driving efficiency and accuracy in manufacturing settings.</p></p>
