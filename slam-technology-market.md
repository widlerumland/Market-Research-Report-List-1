<p><ul><li>Visual SLAM</li><li>Laser SLAM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping) technology encompasses different market types, primarily visual SLAM and laser SLAM. Visual SLAM relies on cameras to capture visual data, enabling devices to map their surroundings and track their position based on image features. In contrast, laser SLAM uses LiDAR (Light Detection and Ranging) sensors to create precise 3D maps by measuring distances with laser pulses. Both types are vital in robotics, augmented reality, and autonomous vehicles, enhancing navigation and environmental understanding.</p></p>
