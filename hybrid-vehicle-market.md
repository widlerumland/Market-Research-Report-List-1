<p><ul><li>Parallel Hybrid Vehicle</li><li>Series Hybrid Vehicle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hybrid vehicle market is primarily divided into parallel and series hybrids. In a parallel hybrid, both the internal combustion engine (ICE) and electric motor can drive the vehicle, allowing for efficient energy use and flexibility in power delivery. Conversely, series hybrids primarily rely on the electric motor for propulsion, with the ICE functioning only as a generator to recharge the battery. Both types aim to enhance fuel efficiency, reduce emissions, and provide a more sustainable transportation solution.</p></p>
