<p><p>Banjo fittings are specialized components used in fluid transfer systems, characterized by their unique configurations. Linear banjo fittings allow for straightforward piping connections, ideal for simple applications. Elbow banjo fittings create a 90-degree angle, aiding in tight space installations. T-shaped banjo fittings enable the branching of fluids at a right angle, facilitating multiple flow paths. Y-shaped banjo fittings allow for a diverging fluid flow, providing flexibility in connecting different lines while maintaining optimal fluid dynamics in various systems.</p></p>
