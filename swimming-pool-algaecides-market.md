<p><p>The swimming pool algaecides market includes several types: Quat pool algaecides utilize quaternary ammonium compounds for effective algae control. Polymer/Polyquat pool algaecides offer a less harsh alternative, providing residual protection without foaming. Metallic pool algaecides leverage metal-based formulas, such as copper compounds, which inhibit algae growth. Sodium bromide is another option that acts as a sanitizer and algicide, effectively preventing algae bloom in various water conditions. Each type caters to specific needs and preferences in pool maintenance.</p></p>
