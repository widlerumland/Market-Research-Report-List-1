<p><p>The Battery-Free RFID Sensor market can be categorized into three main types based on frequency: Low Frequency (LF), High Frequency (HF), and Ultra High Frequency (UHF). LF sensors operate typically at 125-134 kHz and are ideal for short-range applications such as animal tracking. HF sensors, operating at 13.56 MHz, are used in applications like access control and contactless payments. UHF sensors, ranging from 300 MHz to 960 MHz, offer longer read ranges and are suitable for inventory management and supply chain logistics. Each type serves specific industries and applications, enhancing efficiency and reducing maintenance needs.</p></p>
