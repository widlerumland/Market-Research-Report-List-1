Pure Polyurea coatings are known for their high resilience, rapid curing, and superior waterproofing properties, making them ideal for industrial and commercial applications. Hybrid Polyurea, on the other hand, combines polyurea with polyaspartic components to enhance flexibility and durability, suitable for both residential and light commercial use. Each type offers unique advantages, catering to different needs in flooring solutions.</p></p>
