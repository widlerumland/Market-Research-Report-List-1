<p><strong>The Automotive Bipolar Transistors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NPN</li><li>PNP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive bipolar transistors market is primarily divided into two types: NPN and PNP transistors. NPN transistors are widely used in automotive applications for switching and amplification due to their efficient electron mobility, making them ideal for power management and signal amplification. Conversely, PNP transistors are utilized for similar purposes but are primarily activated by negative voltage, making them suitable for high-side switching scenarios. Both types are crucial for modern automotive electronics, including engine control units, power steering, and lighting systems.</p></p>
