<p><strong>The Liquid Film Photoresist Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Photoresist</li><li>Negative Photoresist</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid film photoresist market is predominantly categorized into two types: positive photoresist and negative photoresist. Positive photoresist becomes soluble when exposed to light, allowing selective removal of exposed areas to create intricate patterns. It's favored for high-resolution applications. Conversely, negative photoresist hardens upon exposure, rendering the unexposed areas removable, which makes it suitable for applications requiring robust features. Both types play crucial roles in semiconductor manufacturing and photolithography, influencing the advancement of electronic and microfabrication technologies.</p></p>
