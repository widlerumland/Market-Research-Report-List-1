<p><p>The Heavy Oil Burner market features three main types: Single Stage Burners, Two Stage Burners, and Modulating Burners. Single Stage Burners operate at a fixed output, providing consistent flame intensity, making them suitable for simple applications. Two Stage Burners offer two levels of output, allowing for better efficiency and reduced emissions during varying load conditions. Modulating Burners continuously adjust their combustion output based on demand, maximizing efficiency and minimizing fuel consumption, making them ideal for dynamic operational environments.</p></p>
